# Indian eCommerce Orders Data Analysis Dashboard

##  Project Overview
The Indian eCommerce Orders Data Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer purchasing patterns, and regional sales distribution. The dashboard enables users to explore business performance through dynamic visualizations and interactive filters, helping identify key trends and support decision-making.

---

##  Objective
- Analyze overall sales, profit, and quantity sold.
- Identify top-performing product categories and sub-categories.
- Compare sales and profit across different payment methods.
- Analyze sales distribution by state and city.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business insights.

---

##  Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard development
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
- **DAX** – KPI calculations and measures
- **CSV Files** – Data source

---

##  Dataset
The dashboard is built using two datasets:

- **Orders.csv** – Contains order-related information such as customer, city, state, and order details.
- **Details.csv** – Contains sales amount, profit, quantity, category, sub-category, and payment mode information.

---

##  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Sales:** The business generated **₹2M** in total sales.
- **Total Quantity Sold:** A total of **6K products** were sold.
- **Total Profit:** The business earned an overall profit of **₹37K**.
- **Average Order Amount:** Customers spent an average of **₹291.85** per order.

---

##  Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards for Total Sales, Total Quantity, Total Profit, and Average Order Amount.
- Donut Chart showing Sales by Category.
- Column Chart comparing Sales and Profit by Payment Mode.
- Map visualization displaying Sales by State.
- Bar Chart showing Sales by Sub-Category.
- Treemap highlighting the Top 10 Cities by Sales.
- Interactive slicers for Category, Payment Mode, Sub-Category, City, and State.

---

##  Business Insights
- **Electronics** generated the highest sales with approximately **₹819K (37%)**, followed by **Clothing** with **₹688K (32%)** and **Furniture** with **₹666K (31%)**.
- The business generated **₹2M** in total sales while earning an overall profit of **₹37K**.
- Approximately **6K products** were sold, with an average customer order value of **₹291.85**.
- **Cash on Delivery (COD)** was the leading payment method based on sales, followed by Credit Card, EMI, UPI, and Debit Card.
- The **Top 10 cities** contributed a significant share of total sales, with **Indore, Mumbai, Mathura, Pune, and Delhi** among the top-performing locations.
- Sales were recorded across multiple Indian states, indicating a broad customer base and market reach.
- Interactive filters allow users to analyze business performance by Category, Payment Mode, Sub-Category, City, and State.
